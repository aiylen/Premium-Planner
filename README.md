MINI PLANNER — Premium Digital Planner
===================================================
Thank you for your purchase! Here's everything you need to get started.

HOW TO OPEN IT
---------------------------------------------------
1. Unzip this folder anywhere on your computer.
2. Double-click "index.html".
3. It opens straight in your browser — no installation, no account,
   no sign-up. That's it, you're in.

Works in Chrome, Safari, Edge and Firefox, on desktop, tablet or phone.
An internet connection is needed the first time you open it (to load
a couple of fonts and chart graphics) — after that it works offline.

WHAT'S INSIDE
---------------------------------------------------
Your planner opens with sample demo data already filled in, so you can
see every feature working right away:

  Dashboard · Daily Planner · Weekly Planner · Monthly Planner
  Goal Planner · Habit Tracker · Finance Tracker · Meal Planner
  Water Tracker · Self-Care Planner · Notes

Plus: 5 color themes, dark mode, auto-save, and print/PDF export.

MAKING IT YOURS
---------------------------------------------------
When you're ready to stop looking at demo data and start using your
own, click "Reset Demo Data" in the sidebar. It will ask for your name
and then clear everything to a blank slate — your name will show up
on the dashboard from then on.

Everything you type auto-saves to your browser as you go, so you can
close the tab and come back anytime — it'll be exactly as you left it.
(It's saved locally in your browser, so reopening on a different
device or browser starts fresh there.)

NEED HELP?
---------------------------------------------------
Check the included User Guide PDF (in the "pdf" folder) for a full
walkthrough of every feature. If something looks wrong, please reach
out through the shop you purchased this from — happy to help.

Enjoy your new planner!
